Pelita Air Trade Union Application

A web-based application developed to support the Trade Union organization of Pelita Air in managing member data, administration, and internal union activities efficiently.

This system helps streamline union management processes such as membership management, documentation, communication, and reporting.

Overview

The Pelita Air Trade Union Application was built to digitalize administrative processes within the trade union organization.
It provides a centralized system for managing union members, handling internal records, and improving operational transparency.

The goal of this system is to simplify administrative work while ensuring that union data is well-organized and accessible.

Features

Member registration and management

Member profile and status tracking

Administrative document management

Announcement and information sharing

Internal reporting system

Dashboard for union management

Data search and filtering

Secure user authentication

System Modules
Member Management

Manage trade union members including:

Personal information

Employment details

Membership status

Contact information

Administration

Manage internal union documentation and administrative records.

Announcements

Provide updates and important announcements to union members.

Reporting

Generate reports related to membership and union activities.
